Prison Ministry

Woodleaf Baptist Church started a prison ministry at Piedmont Correctional Institute in 1999. We visit weekly and thousands of people have been reached behind bars with the news of God's love.

Through this ministry, we have witnessed many changed lives and we have restored families.

Card Ministry

Member of our Card Ministry make and send cards who are in the hospital or are going through a rough time

Through this ministry, we have raised the spirits of many people.

Rowan Bible Teachers Association

The goal of the West Rowan Bible Teaching Association is to support the non-denominational Bible teaching classes in the public schools of Western Rowan County. Support for this program comes through donations and sponsored community events.

The teachers in this program are paid strictly from the funds raised through these donations and community events. These funds cover their salaries and health care insurance.
