Read below to learn about our current events

Ladies' Group

The Ladies' Group is organized by the wonderful, Mrs Bea. Each a month, a group of women come together to casually hang out and converse about their faith.

This event occurs monthly, usually on a Monday. Please contact us to find out when the next time the ladies' group will meet.

Men's Group

Once a month a group of men get together to have breakfast together and afterwards, they have a time of devotion. We encourage and welcome all men to come out and join this group.

This event occurs monthly, usually on a Saturday. Please contact us to find out when the next time the men's group will meet.
