Woodleaf Baptist: Our History

Woodleaf Baptist Church

7790 Woodleaf Rd

Woodleaf, North Carolina 27054

Read below to learn about our history

At Woodleaf Baptist, we pride ourselves on being an old-fashioned, country church. Our goal as a church is to follow to the Lords’s will and to help the people around us. To achieve our goal of helping others, we are involved in the community with ministries to the local prison, homeless shelter and multiple nursing homes. Our services are casual dress because our goal is the condition of your heart before God, not your wardrobe before men. With God’s help, we joyfully face each day with a smile and looking forward to our Lord’s return.

Woodleaf Baptist Church was founded and built in 1978. Our church was build ith only a handful of people that felt the Spirit of God leading them to build a place where people can come together and worship the Lord. In 1978, there was no Baptist church in our section of Rowan County, so we pride ourselves on being the first Baptist church in our area. Through the years the faces of our church have changed, but our heart has remained the same.

In August of 1991, Reverend Chris Williams joined us as our pastor and he continues to serve our church family and this community to the present time.
